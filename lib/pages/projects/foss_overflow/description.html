Foss Overflow 2024

Foss Overflow is a initiative by IIT Bhilai for developers to collaborate on open-source projects. It is designed to provide a supportive environment for developers who are new to the open source scene and help them to learn, grow, and contribute to the open-source community. Foss Overflow 2024 is the latest iteration of the event, and I was a part of a three member team that developed the amazing all in one Smart Insti App.

Smart Insti App

The Smart Insti App is an all in one app for student and teachers alike. It provides a variety of features such as a Lost and Found, Room Vacancy, Chatrooms, Mess Menu. Making the everyday life of students and teachers easier. The repository and the app itself are made in such a way that it can be easily adapted and used by any institute with minor changes.
