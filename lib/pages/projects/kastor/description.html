Kastor - Your Ultimate Game Price Comparison Website

Welcome to "Kastor," a powerful game price comparison website designed to help gamers find the best deals on their favorite titles. Derived from the term "beaver," which is known for its resourcefulness and digging expertise, Kastor is your go-to platform for scouting game prices across various vendors, including Steam, Origin, Epic Games, EA Games, Battle.net, Kinguin, and many more. Just like Trivago, but exclusively for games, Kastor promises to change the way you shop for video games online.

Key Features:

  • Game Price Scraping:

    • Kastor employs sophisticated Python scripts to scrape game prices from numerous vendors, ensuring that you always have access to the latest and most competitive deals.

  • Vendor Comparison:

    • With Kastor, you can effortlessly compare prices from different vendors side by side, helping you make informed decisions on where to purchase your desired games.

  • User Account Registration:

    • Create your personalized account on Kastor to unlock additional features and receive tailored recommendations based on your gaming preferences.

  • Wishlist Management:

    • Stay organized with Kastor's built-in wishlist feature, where you can save games you plan to buy in the future. Receive notifications when the prices of your wishlist games drop, ensuring you never miss a great deal.

  • Intuitive User Interface:

    • Kastor boasts an elegant and user-friendly interface, making game browsing and price comparisons a breeze for gamers of all levels.

Technologies Used:

Kastor is built using a powerful combination of HTML, CSS, PHP, and Bootstrap, ensuring a seamless and responsive user experience across various devices and platforms. Python scripts are integrated into the backend to perform web scraping, ensuring the most up-to-date game prices from multiple sources.
