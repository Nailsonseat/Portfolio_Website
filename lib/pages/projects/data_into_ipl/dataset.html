Dataset Descriptions

  • Ball by Ball dataset - This dataset contains every ball bowled with bowler mapped to batter, along with details like runs scored, innings, batting team, etc.

    Features:
    • ID: Unique identifier for each match.
    • innings: The inning number (1 or 2).
    • overs: The over number within the inning.
    • ballnumber: The ball number within the over.
    • batter: The name of the batter facing the ball.
    • bowler: The name of the bowler delivering the ball.
    • non-striker: The name of the non-striking batter.
    • extra_type: Type of extra (e.g., wide, no-ball), if any.
    • batsman_run: Runs scored by the batter off that ball.
    • extras_run: Extra runs conceded off that ball.
    • total_run: Total runs scored off that ball (batsman_run + extras_run).
    • non_boundary: Indicates if the run(s) scored were not from a boundary.
    • isWicketDelivery: Indicates if the ball resulted in a wicket.
    • player_out: Name of the player who got out, if any.
    • kind: Type of dismissal (e.g., bowled, caught).
    • fielders_involved: Names of fielders involved in the dismissal.
    • BattingTeam: The team batting in that inning.

  • Match Result dataset - This dataset contains the result of every match played in the IPL, who won, who lost and by what margin.

    Features:
    • ID: Unique identifier for each match.
    • City: The city where the match was played.
    • Date: The date on which the match was played.
    • Season: The IPL season (year).
    • MatchNumber: The match number within the season.
    • Team1: The first team playing the match.
    • Team2: The second team playing the match.
    • Venue: The venue where the match was played.
    • TossWinner: The team that won the toss.
    • TossDecision: The decision made by the toss winner (bat or field).
    • SuperOver: Indicates if the match had a Super Over.
    • WinningTeam: The team that won the match.
    • WonBy: The method by which the match was won (runs or wickets).
    • Margin: The margin of victory (number of runs or wickets).
    • method: The method of result determination, if any (e.g., DLS method).
    • Player_of_Match: The player awarded the Player of the Match.
    • Team1Players: List of players in Team1.
    • Team2Players: List of players in Team2.
    • Umpire1: Name of the first umpire.
    • Umpire2: Name of the second umpire.

  • Player dataset - This dataset contains the details of every player who has played in the IPL, and which team do they play for.

    Features:
    • player: Name of the player.
    • Team: The team the player is associated with.
