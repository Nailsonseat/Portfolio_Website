Holomorphic Bot: E-Yantra 22-23

The Holomorphic Bot is an innovative and versatile tri-wheeler robot developed as part of the prestigious E Yantra 22-23 competition. Designed with movable limbs and wheels positioned 120 degrees apart, the Holomorphic Bot operates in a wide range of degrees of freedom, making it highly adaptable to diverse environments and tasks. The project leverages ROS Noetic Ninjemys and Gazebo, to enable seamless simulation and robust performance of the robot in real life scenarios.
