ONDC Build for Bharat

Overview

The Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC) is a government initiative to create a unified digital commerce platform for the country. The platform aims to provide a level playing field for all participants in the digital commerce ecosystem, including buyers, sellers, and service providers. The ONDC platform will enable small and medium businesses to reach a wider audience and compete with larger players in the market. It will also provide consumers with a wide range of choices and a seamless shopping experience.



Aim

The aim of the ONDC Build for Bharat hackathon is to develop a innovative solution that will be able to help shop owners to list their products or entire catalogues on the ONDC platform so that those products can be discovered by a wider audience irrespective of the seller app or website. The solution should be able to help the shop owners to manage their inventory, orders, and payments in a seamless manner.



Problem Statement

The challenge is to develop innovative solutions that leverage cutting-edge technologies to seamlessly digitalize and enhance product catalogs, offering a user-friendly experience for sellers and seller apps. Consider a sample catalog with at least 1000 SKUs, with attributes such as SKU id, product name, description, price, image, inventory, colour, size, brand, etc. This catalog is to be digitized using a combination of intuitive interfaces such as text / voice / image input, with text & voice input using any of the Indic languages. In some cases, a combination of these interfaces are required to digitize an SKU e.g. scan image which pre-fills the product name from the repository, with the remaining attributes filled using text or voice input.



Solution Expected & Deliverables

Showcase a solution that allows catalog digitization using a combination of interfaces as explained above. Provide a mechanism to measure the throughput of digitization (for e.g x number of catalog digitisation in one day). All artefact used should be elaborated. All assumptions should be elaborated.
