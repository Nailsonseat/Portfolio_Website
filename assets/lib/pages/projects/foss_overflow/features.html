Students and Faculties

Currently Students and faculty enjoy the same set of features as far as out joint proposal was concerned. It is only in the future when the roles will be more defined and the features will be more role specific.
  1. Lost and Found

    2. This feature is for both students and faculties. It is a platform where students and faculties can list items that they have lost or found. This is a very important feature as it will help in reuniting people with their lost items. This feature is also very important for the faculty as they can list items that they have found in their classrooms and students can claim them. There is also a launch dialer to instantly input the number without the need of copy pasting.

  2. Room Vacancy

    3. Room Vacancy is a feature that is for every role. It is a feature to check if the rooms are vacant and reserve them if they are. This is a very important feature for the students as they can reserve rooms for their meetings and group studies. This is also important for the faculty as they can reserve rooms for their classes and meetings.

  3. Chatrooms (Done by another team member)

    4. Chatrooms is a feature that is for every role. It is a feature to chat with other students and faculties. This is a very important feature for the students as they can chat with their classmates and faculties. This is also important for the faculty as they can chat with their students.

  4. Mess Menu

    5. Mess Menu is a feature that is for every role. It is a feature to check the mess menu. This is a very important feature for the students as they can check the mess menu and decide whether to go to the mess or not.

  5. TimeTables

    6. TimeTables is a feature that is for students and faculties. It is a feature to check the time tables. This is a very important feature for the users as they can check their time tables and plan their day accordingly. The time tables are basically organized in a landscape grid layout which is entirely zoomable. The most hard to implement feature of the entire project.
